-- ENGLISH VERSION --

Today I completely disassembled the first prototype made from a cardboard box and reassembled everything inside an IKEA plastic box.
plastic box from IKEA.

I took the opportunity to document each piece of the mechanism with a photo. the photos can be found in the
photos

Afterwards, during reassembly, I worked on perfecting the drop system driven by the second servo motor.
After various trials and errors, I arrived at a functioning version of the mechanism, also thanks to the change of the servo power supply. Previously, the servo took power from the arduino and did not have enough power, whereas now it is connected to the stepper motor  management chip and therefore also has to be powered by the relais

solved the problem of the motor making strange sounds by setting the default step number to a power of 2
from 2000 it is now 2048

pierced the cap of the kettle in order to be able to insert the pipe carrying the water with the pump

among other things I set up git authentication via ssh and started journaling... even if late
